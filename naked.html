KTM 790 DUKE THE SCALPEL

The KTM 790 DUKE is devastatingly accurate. The agility you’d expect from a single, blended with the meaty punch of a twin. It carves corners with pinpoint precision, boasting the all-new 799 cc LC8c parallel twin motor nestled into one of the lightest and most compact chassis around. The KTM 790 DUKE is all about being fast where it counts – in the corners. Dynamically flicking from one bend to the next, putting you in the heart of the action and not on the sidelines.

KTM 690 DUKE THE SUPER SINGLE

The original DUKE was revolutionary when it was released two decades ago. As KTM’s first single-cylinder street bike, it quickly grew into a cult classic, adding extreme fun to a raw and radical concept. Today’s KTM 690 DUKE is the closest rendition to the original DUKE, but with modern styling, refinements and enhancements. The world’s most powerful single-cylinder production motorcycle stands at the leading edge once more. Long live the DUKE!

KTM 200 DUKE THE LIGHT HEAVYWEIGHT

It rides faster than it looks. This fighter is the only 200cc naked bike with real racing genes. KTM 125 DUKE THE DUKE ATTITUDE STARTS HERE

The KTM 125 DUKE may be the entry-level bike of the range, but it isn't here to play games. It's built with the same attitude as the bigger bikes - and it shows.
